Play cards.
Learn how to code.

Code Cards is a card game that teaches you how to
code whilst allowing you to go outside, have fun with
friends or just take a break.

...it's free & open source!

Question on the front,
answers on the back.

Cards can have numbers from 1-9 and be 1
of 4 colors. There are also 3 power-ups per
color and 3 multicolor cards per deck.

Play it like Uno! if you get
it right.

Every player starts with 7 random cards
and must match the card on top of the
discard pile with either the same number
or color. You can only discard a card if you
get the answer right though!
